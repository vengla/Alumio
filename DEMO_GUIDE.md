## 🎯 3-Minute Demo Flow

### Part 1: Web Application (2 minutes)

1. **Open your browser** and navigate to: `http://localhost:3000`

2. **Landing Page** - See:
   - Professional hero section with gradient
   - Stats showcase (12K+ alumni, 850+ jobs)
   - 6 feature cards (Directory, Mentorship, Jobs, Donations, Stories, Events)
   - Call-to-action buttons

3. **Click "Login"** (top-right)
   - Select role: **Alumni** | Student | Admin
   - Enter any email/password (demo mode)
   - Click **Login**

4. **Dashboard** (Alumni View)
   - 4 stat cards: Network (145), Mentees (3), Job Posts (2), Donations (₹25K)
   - Quick actions grid
   - Recent activity feed
   - Upcoming events

5. **Click "Directory"** in navigation
   - Search bar with filters (Batch, Department, Location)
   - 6 alumni cards with avatars, roles, companies
   - Mentor availability badges
   - "Connect" buttons

6. **Click "Jobs"** in navigation
   - Filter chips (All, Full-time, Internship)
   - 6 job listings with:
     - Referral badges
     - Salary ranges
     - Posted by alumni info
   - "Apply Now" buttons

7. **Click "Donations"** in navigation
   - Hero banner with total raised (₹2.5Cr)
   - 4 donation categories with progress bars
   - Donation form with preset amounts
   - Fund utilization transparency dashboard
   - Hall of Donors section

8. **Try Admin Role**
   - Go back to Login
   - Select **Admin** role
   - Dashboard shows:
     - Pending approvals (23)
     - Analytics tabs
     - User/Job/Story approval queues
